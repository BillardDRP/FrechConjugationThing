What does it all mean?

Avoir: "To Have" ie: J'ai soixante ans; I have 60 years of age.

Être: "To be" ie: Ils sont tres intelligent!; They are very inteligent.

Aller: "To go"

Faire: Mainly used as to make ie: je te fais un café? Can I make you a coffe? or to do ie: Est-ce que tu as fait tes devoirs? Have you done your homework?
